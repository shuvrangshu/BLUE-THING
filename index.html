HOW IT ALL BEGAN

We, some of the students of Carmel High School Kolkata, while working on a collaborative project with schools of eastern India and Kentucky, USA first came in touch with some of the local surface water bodies namely Jodhpur Park Lake and Poddarnagar Lake (also called Katjunagar Pond). While working on the water quality of these two said water bodies we came to realize that these surface water bodies of Kolkata influence the lives of the local inhabitants in so many ways yet these are neglected and not given the attention they deserve. This motivated us to continue our work on these surface water bodies.
